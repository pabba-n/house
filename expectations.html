House Expectations

  • If you're having more than 4 people over give a heads up
  • Give a heads up a good amount in advance if you're gonna have more than 10 people over (no ragers tho 🥹)

Split utilities equally. If utilities are ever higher than usual, we will discuss it to see how to reduce costs for the next month.

  • Vacuuming - vacuum kitchen, living room, dining room, stairs, and upstairs hallway
  • Wiping - wipe surfaces in kitchen, living room, and dining room with disinfectant
  • Trash - wheel the trash bin from the garage to the curb on Sunday nights for trash day

  • Take trash out of kitchen whenever it is full to the trash bin in the garage
  • Rinse your dish and put it in the dishwasher immediately
  • Turn on the dishwasher if you put in the last dish before it is full
  • Take dishes out of dishwasher if you see they've been cleaned

Deep clean bathrooms once a semester

We'll split eggs, oil, rice, butter, spices and condiments

Generally split them and speak up if you feel like you've been dealing with a lot of them them!

We should all have designated shelf spaces

We are a no shoes house, so try to take shoes off at the top of the stairs. If you need to grab something really quick with shoes on that's fine.

It's ok to make noise between 9am-11:30pm. Outside of these hours, please keep the noise down.
